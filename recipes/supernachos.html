Super Nachos

Description

This makes a huge meal-sized tray of nachos with lots of good stuff! You can adjust ingredient quantities to suit your preference. Serve with extra chips if required. Great for game day.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tortills chips
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Set the oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the broiler. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Broil in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, watching carefully to prevent burning, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Top nachos with salsa, sour cream, black olives, green onions, and jalapeno peppers.
